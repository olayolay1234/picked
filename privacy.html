Privacy & Policy

At Pet lover, we understand the importance of protecting your privacy and ensuring the safety of your personal and pet-related information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.
1. Information We Collect
o Personal Information

When you book services, create an account, or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and billing details.

o pet Information

We collect details about your pet(s), such as breed, age, medical history, and special needs, to provide the best care.

o Payment Information

We use third-party payment processors to handle payments securely. We do not store credit card information ourselves.

2. How We Use Your Information

o To provide pet care services such as boarding, grooming, training, and feeding.

o To process payments and send receipts or invoices.

o To communicate with you regarding service updates, appointments, or promotional offers related to pet care.

o To improve our services based on your feedback and interactions.

3. Data Security

o We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal and pet-related information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

o All payment transactions are processed securely through trusted third-party vendors. We do not store your payment card details.

4. Sharing Your Information

o We will not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

o We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in providing our services.

A pet lover is someone who has a deep affection for animals and treats them as a cherished part of the family. Whether it's dogs, cats, or exotic pets, pet lovers are dedicated to providing the best care, attention, and love to their furry, feathery, or scaly friends.
